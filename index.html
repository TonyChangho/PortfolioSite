Tony Changho

About

Grammy-nominated singer, Olympic-caliber athlete, all-American hero: these are the things Tony Changho is not. However, he IS a seasoned software engineer with nine years of career experience in front-end web development and a passion for all things related to the web. Through his talent and expertise, he's had the privelege to work on projects for icons such as Nike, Viacom, Mastercard, GE, Fidelity, Gust, and even Snoop Dogg, and is always looking for the next great project.
